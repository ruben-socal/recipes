Ingriedients:
-1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
-1 cup vegetable oil
-3 eggs
-2 cups Gold Medal™ all-purpose flour
-2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
-1 teaspoon baking soda
-1 teaspoon vanilla
-1/2 teaspoon salt
-3 cups shredded carrots (5 medium)
-1 cup coarsely chopped walnuts

Directions:

  In large bowl, beat granulated sugar, oil and eggs with electric mixer on low speed about 30   seconds or until blended. Add flour, cinnamon, baking soda, 1 teaspoon vanilla and the salt;   beat on low speed 1 minute. Stir in carrots and nuts.
